Help Step 1 User need to select the design and quantity. Step 2 On the 'My Cart' page, the user must select the booking date. Then press the checkout button to confirm. Step 3 After that, continue by clicking the 'checkout' button to confirm the order and booking date. Step 4 Finally, click the 'payment' button to make the payment. Step 5 And then, a message will appear to show that your reservation has been confirmed and has been sent to the admin. Step 6 Finally, the user's order details will be displayed on the "My Orders" page.
